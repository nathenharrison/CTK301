project 3

Our game is a 2-4 player pvp puzzle game where players compete to reach the center first. The players have 3 ships to manuever around the board while avoiding the other players bomb placements and attacks. Each ship has a different attribute that can effect the game. Small ships have the abilities to pass by large mines, Medium ships have the ability to destroy the mines and the Big ship is a tanky ship that can take plenty of damage. Players turns will consist of deciding what to do with each ship and there is only take one action per ship
